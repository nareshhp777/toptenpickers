Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Top10, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Voluntarily provided information such as your name and email address when you contact us via our contact form.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our website
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our content
  • To serve personalized advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

Top10 uses Google AdSense, a web advertising service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Google's advertising cookies (including the former DoubleClick DART cookie) allow Google and its partner advertisers to serve ads to you based on your visit to Top10 and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings (adssettings.google.com). You may also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom may also manage advertising cookies through the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA).

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites that are not owned or controlled by Top10. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of every site that collects personal information before providing any data to them.

Data Security & Retention

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

International Data Transfers

Top10 is operated from India. By using our website, you understand that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or in other countries where our service providers operate. We take steps to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy regardless of where it is processed.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA & Global Privacy)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or update of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request data portability in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (California / CCPA)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We respond to all verified requests within 30 days.
