Top 10 Electric Sports Bikes of 2026

Ranked by 0-60 mph times, battery range, and EV engineering

The electric motorcycle revolution has matured into a full-blown performance war. In 2026, the boundaries between internal combustion and battery-electric sportbikes have all but disappeared — and in many cases, electric has taken the lead. Instant torque delivery, sophisticated traction control, and energy-dense battery packs now produce machines that out-accelerate, out-corner, and increasingly out-range their gasoline rivals. From boutique American manufacturers to European exotics and Asian mass-market players, here is our ranking of the ten most thrilling electric sports bikes you can ride or order today.

  1. Lightning LS-218

    0-60 mph: 2.2s Top Speed: 218 mph Range: 188 mi Power: 244 hp

    The LS-218 is the original electric superbike and remains the fastest production motorcycle in the world. Its IPM liquid-cooled motor delivers an astonishing 244 horsepower and 168 lb-ft of torque, propelling the bike to a verified 218 mph at the Bonneville Salt Flats. With a 380V, 20 kWh battery pack, the LS-218 offers up to 188 miles of city range and features Öhlins suspension, Brembo brakes, and a single-sided swingarm. It is the benchmark every other electric sportbike aspires to beat.

  2. Damon HyperSport Premier

    0-60 mph: 2.4s Top Speed: 200 mph Range: 200 mi Power: 200 hp

    The Damon HyperSport redefines what a sportbike can be with its groundbreaking Shift technology, which physically transforms the riding position from sport to commuter in milliseconds. Powered by a 21 kWh battery and a 200 hp permanent magnet motor, the HyperSport Premier offers a 200-mile highway range, advanced 360° collision warning, and Level 2 autonomy. It is the smartest motorcycle ever built.

  3. Energica Ego+ RS

    0-60 mph: 2.6s Top Speed: 150 mph Range: 261 mi Power: 171 hp

    The Italian-built Energica Ego+ RS is the sole motorcycle used in the MotoE World Championship, where it has proven its durability and performance against the world's best riders. Its updated 21.5 kWh battery delivers an incredible 261-mile combined range, and the new RS-spec motor produces 171 hp. Fast-charge capability refills the pack to 80% in just 40 minutes at any CCS station.

  4. Zero SR/S

    0-60 mph: 3.0s Top Speed: 124 mph Range: 187 mi Power: 113 hp

    The Zero SR/S combines aggressive sportbike ergonomics with touring capability. Its full fairing reduces drag by 13% compared to the naked SR/F, extending highway range to 124 miles. Cypher III+ operating system enables over-the-air updates, customizable performance maps, and integrated Bosch cornering ABS. It is the most refined electric sportbike for real-world riders.

  5. Verge TS Pro

    0-60 mph: 2.5s Top Speed: 124 mph Range: 217 mi Power: 137 hp

    The Verge TS Pro is a Finnish engineering marvel featuring an in-hub electric motor integrated directly into the rear wheel. This radical design eliminates the need for chains, belts, or gears, and frees up the entire frame to house a massive battery. The result is a hubless-rear-wheel aesthetic that turns heads and delivers 137 hp with stunning throttle response.

  6. LiveWire S2 Del Mar

    0-60 mph: 3.0s Top Speed: 103 mph Range: 113 mi Power: 84 hp

    Harley-Davidson's LiveWire spin-off created the S2 Del Mar as its first mass-market electric motorcycle. Built on the new ARROW architecture, it is 50% lighter than the original LiveWire ONE yet still delivers a thrilling 84 hp. Its modular battery system, 100+ dealer network, and iconic American design make it the most accessible premium electric sportbike.

  7. Arc Vector

    0-60 mph: 3.2s Top Speed: 124 mph Range: 271 mi Power: 117 hp

    The Arc Vector is the most exclusive electric motorcycle ever made. Limited to just 399 units worldwide, it features a carbon-fiber monocoque chassis, hub-center steering, and a heads-up display helmet with a neural-input wrist controller. Its 21.7 kWh battery delivers 271 miles of range, and the bike can be customized by the customer at Arc's Norfolk, UK facility.

  8. Sondors Metacycle

    0-60 mph: 4.5s Top Speed: 80 mph Range: 80 mi Power: 20 hp

    The Sondors Metacycle is the most affordable electric sportbike in the world that still looks like one. Its minimalist cast-magnesium frame, exposed battery, and naked roadster styling make it a favorite among urban commuters. With 80 miles of range and a removable battery that charges in any household outlet, the Metacycle has democratized EV two-wheelers.

  9. Ryvid Anthem

    0-60 mph: 4.0s Top Speed: 75 mph Range: 75 mi Power: 27 hp

    The Ryvid Anthem is a California-designed lightweight electric sportbike featuring a unique rotating chassis that lets riders adjust the seat height from 30 to 34 inches at the touch of a button. Its 4.3 kWh removable battery, reverse gear, and 75-mile range make it a practical urban bike with serious attitude. The aircraft-grade aluminum frame keeps weight to just 240 lbs.

  10. Super73-C1X

    0-60 mph: 5.5s Top Speed: 65 mph Range: 75 mi Power: 22 hp

    The Super73-C1X blurs the line between electric bicycle and motorcycle. Designed for street-legal use without a motorcycle license in most US states, it features motorcycle-grade suspension, hydraulic disc brakes, and a 2 kWh battery. The C1X retains Super73's signature moto-inspired design while delivering genuine sportbike performance for urban riders.

Final Thoughts

The electric sports bike category in 2026 is defined by astonishing variety. At the apex, the Lightning LS-218 still rules with its 218 mph top speed, but bikes like the Damon HyperSport and Energica Ego+ RS are proving that electric motorcycles can be every bit as thrilling, plus more practical for daily use. As battery technology continues to improve and charging infrastructure expands, expect the next 24 months to deliver even more disruptive machines. The future of two-wheeled performance is electric — and it's arrived.
