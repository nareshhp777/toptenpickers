About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Top10 — your definitive source for curated rankings across the world's most exciting categories. From hypercars and luxury yachts to cutting-edge gadgets and record-breaking aircraft, we research, rank, and review the very best in every field.

Our Story

Top10 was born from a passion for excellence. We noticed that most "top 10" lists online were shallow, biased, or outdated. So we built a platform that treats rankings with the seriousness they deserve — combining hard performance data with the kind of detail that true enthusiasts crave.

What started as a single list of the world's top cars has grown into a growing library of rankings spanning multiple categories including automotive, technology, mobile gaming, and more. We're committed to expanding our coverage while maintaining the depth and accuracy that sets us apart.

Our Editorial Process

Every Top 10 list on this site follows a documented, repeatable research process so you can evaluate our work:

  1. Candidate identification — We define a category and assemble a longlist from official manufacturer sources, industry publications, expert reviews, and verified consumer feedback.
  2. Data collection — We collect verified performance specifications, pricing, expert consensus, and real-world testing data from authoritative sources.
  3. Scoring — Each entry is scored against consistent criteria (e.g., performance, value, design, innovation) using a transparent weighted rubric.
  4. Ranking & fact-check — Entries are ranked by aggregate score. Every spec, price, and claim is fact-checked against the manufacturer's official data before publication.
  5. Periodic review — Published lists are reviewed and updated on a regular schedule to reflect new product releases, spec revisions, and market changes.

What We Stand For

  • Accuracy — Every spec is verified from official sources.
  • Independence — Our rankings are unbiased and editorially driven.
  • Depth — We don't just list names; we explain why each entry belongs.
  • Transparency — Affiliate links are clearly disclosed (see our Disclaimer).
